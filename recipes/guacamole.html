Guacamole Recipe

Restaurant-worthy guacamole is just a few ingredients away. You'll be surprised how easy it is to make this top-rated guacamole recipe. You'll come back to it again and again!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe hass avocados
  • 2 roma plum tomato, diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 3 tablespoon of chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl
  2. Mix in tomatoes, onion and cilantro
  3. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
