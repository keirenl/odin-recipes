Lasagna Recipe

I adore a lasagna filled with zucchini, mushrooms, spinach and whatever other vegetables you’d like to put in it. However my children do not feel the same way! They just don’t want chunks of veggies in their lasagna. So I compromised.

There are still plenty of healthy ingredients in this vegan lasagna, such as red lentils, tomato sauce, tofu, cashews, spinach and whole grain noodles. And I promise, it’s delicious!

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • 1 slab of firm tofu
  • bag of spinach
  • spices
  • nooch
  • 2 cans of pasta sauce

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 420F
  2. crumble tofu
  3. mix spices and nooch into tofu
  4. put tofu crumble in baking pan
  5. layer noodles on top
  6. pour both cans of sauce on top
  7. sprinkle poo some spinach
  8. put lasagna in oven for 20mins to bake
